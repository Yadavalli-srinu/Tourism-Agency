Rome

Top
  • Why visit: The capital city is a living museum with iconic landmarks like the Colosseum, Roman Forum, Pantheon, and Vatican City (home to St. Peter’s Basilica and the Sistine Chapel)..
  • Best for: History lovers, art enthusiasts, and foodies.

Venice

Top
  • Why Visit: A magical city built on canals, famous for gondola rides, St. Mark’s Basilica, the Grand Canal, and Doge’s Palace.
  • Highlights: Romantic getaways and unique cultural experiences.

Florence(Firenze)

  • Why Visit : The cradle of the Renaissance, with masterpieces by Michelangelo, Botticelli, and Leonardo da Vinci. Highlights include the Uffizi Gallery, Florence Cathedral (Duomo), and Ponte Vecchio.
  • Highlights: Art lovers and architecture buffs.
