Eiffel Tower (La Tour Eiffel)

top
  • Why it’s famous: The most iconic landmark in Paris and one of the most visited monuments in the world.
  • What to do: Take the elevator or stairs to the top for panoramic views, dine in its restaurants, or enjoy the evening light show.
  • Fun Fact: It sparkles for 5 minutes every hour after sunset.

The Louvre Museum (Musée du Louvre)

top
  • Why it’s famous: The world’s largest art museum, home to the Mona Lisa, Venus de Milo, and thousands of masterpieces.
  • What to do: Explore art from ancient to modern times, walk through the glass pyramid entrance.
  • Fun Fact: It was originally a royal palace before becoming a museum.

Notre-Dame Cathedral

  • Why it’s famous: A masterpiece of Gothic architecture and one of the most recognized cathedrals in the world.
  • What to do: Admire its stained glass, visit the towers, or attend a service.
  • Note: Parts of the cathedral are under restoration after the 2019 fire, but it's still a top spot to visit.
