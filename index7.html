Queenstown (South Island)

Top
  • Why visit: Adventure capital of New Zealand.
  • Best for: Bungee jumping, skydiving, jet boating, skiing
  • Stunning views of Lake Wakatipu and the Remarkables mountain range
  • Access to nearby Milford Sound and Fiordland National Park

Rotorua (North Island)

Top
  • Why Visit:A geothermal wonderland and a hub for Māori culture.
  • Highlights:Hot springs, geysers, mud pools, traditional Māori performances, and forest ziplining.
  • Cultural experiences and geothermal exploration.

Aoraki / Mount Cook National Park (South Island)

  • Why Visit :Alpine scenery and stargazing.
  • Highlights: New Zealand’s highest peak, Aoraki / Mount Cook
  • Glacial lakes (Lake Pukaki, Tasman Glacier)
  • Incredible hikes like the Hooker Valley Track
  • Part of the Aoraki Mackenzie International Dark Sky Reserve
